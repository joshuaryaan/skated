About Skate Ed

We believe our skateboarding lessons are able to create the following:

Safe spaces to learn skateboarding

Learning a new sport can seem very daunting, especially if it is heavily male dominated. At Skate Ed, we focus on providing a judgement free space, where you can learn skateboarding safely, at your own pace.

Professional and approachable coaches

Our coaches all have a background in skateboarding, so we can offer tips and tricks that are guaranteed to lead to success. Each coach has their own unique experience with skateboarding, allowing them to connect with a wide range of participants.

A sense of belonging

We are always working towards creating a sense of belonging and community. Skateboarding is a great source of connectedness, and acceptance. The culture has always pushed for people to be individuals, which is an important message.

Opportunities for children and young people

Something that is also at the centre of our objectives, is creating opportunities for children and young people. Whether these opportunities are gained through being a student, or being a coach, we value the importance of leadership within skateboarding.

Opportunities for women

The gender inequality within skateboarding creates a mental barrier for many women and non-binary wanting to give it a go. By offering skateboarding lessons for women, by women, we believe we can start to break down this barrier.

Our Lessons

All of our lessons are suitable for all ages, and all skill levels. Skateboards, safety pads, and helmets are available to borrow at every lesson. You may book ahead, or show up on the day. However, lessons are weather permitting, so please keep an eye on our social media for weather updates.

We also offer private lessons, in school skateboarding lessons, and birthday parties all of which are available to book ahead. If you would like to enquire about any of our lessons, please do not hesitate to send us a message.
