Frequently Asked Questions

What is the youngest age you teach?

It is very dependent on the person. We have run private lessons with kids as young as 4 years old, but they tend to lose focus before the full hour is up.

How do I book a private lesson?

You can book through our website, or send us a message, email, or text. Please let us know if there is a particular coach you want to work with.

Where do you run private lessons?

We usually run them at your local skatepark, but we can run them anywhere with smooth, flat ground.

How do I get information about school lessons?

You can send us a message through our website, as well as via email or text.

Do you have to hire the equipment?

No, this is not an extra cost. We can provide you with all the equipment you need, just please let us know prior to the lesson.

What is the youngest age you take for after school lessons?

Our after school skateboarding lessons are open to year 3 students and above, these are set up to look after kids while their parents are at work.
