Our Lessons

Our lessons are suitable for all ages, and all skill levels. Skateboards, safety pads, and helmets are available to borrow at every lesson. You may book ahead, or show up on the day. However, lessons are weather permitting, so please keep an eye on our social media for weather updates.

Group Skate Lessons

$15.00-$25.00
1-2 Hours

Our group lessons mainly run throughout the weekend, taking place in Newlands and Upper Hutt. During the school holidays we run lessons in the week, and expand on our regular locations.
We are constantly working on providing more lessons for those who identify as female, and non-binary. All of our girls only lessons are suitable for all ages, all skill levels, and anyone who does not identify as male. These lessons are generally free, thank you to our funding providers.

School Lessons

Skate Ed also operates within schools around the Wellington region, we are always eager to get more schools involved with our work. Skateboarding is a great way for young people to develop their confidence, and feel a sense of belonging. It also allows children to express creativity and independence, while under our supervision.
We are able to run skateboarding lessons at your school during lunchtime, class time, or after school. Please get in touch if this is something you would be interested in, we would be happy to provide you with more information.

Birthday Parties

Have you ever wanted a skateboarding themed birthday party? We also offer private bookings for birthday parties, the price depends on your location. We are able to provide portable ramps, enough gear for 15 people, and 2 first aid certified coaches. Please get in touch if this is something you would be interested in, we would be happy to provide you with more information.
