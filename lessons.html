Our Lessons

Our lessons are suitable for all ages, and all skill levels. Skateboards, safety pads, and helmets are available to borrow at every lesson. You may book ahead, or show up on the day. However, lessons are weather permitting, so please keep an eye on our social media for weather updates.

Group Skate Lessons

$20.00
2 Hours

We run group lessons every weekend, which are $20 per person for 2 hours. These lessons take place at Pukehuia Skatepark, Maidstone Max Skatepark, and Waitangi Skatepark. During the school holidays, we also run group lessons every day of the week, expanding on our regular locations.
We are constantly working on providing more frequent lessons for those who identify as women, and non-binary. All of our girls only lessons are suitable for all ages, all skill levels, and anyone who does not identify as male. These lessons are generally free, thank you to our funding providers.

Private Skate Lessons

School Lessons

Skate Ed also operates within schools around Wellington, we are always eager to get more schools involved with our work. We are able to run skateboarding lessons at your school during lunchtime, class time, and after school. Please get in touch if this is something you would be interested in, we would be happy to provide you with more information.

Birthday Parties

Have you ever wanted a skateboarding session as a birthday party? We also offer private bookings for birthday parties, the price depends on your location. We are able to provide portable ramps, 15 skateboards with helmets and safety gear, and first aid certified coaches. Please get in touch if this is something you would be interested in, we would be happy to provide you with more information.
