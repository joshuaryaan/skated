Our Lessons

Suitable for all ages, and all skill levels. Skateboards, safety pads, and helmets are available to borrow at every lesson. Book ahead, or show up on the day. Please note all lessons are weather permitting, so keep an eye on our social media for cancellations.

Group Skate Lessons

Our group lessons run every weekend, these take place at Pukehuia Skatepark, Maidstone Max Skatepark, and Waitangi Skatepark. The price is $20 per person for a 2 hour lesson.

We are constantly working on providing more frequent lessons for women* (and non-binary), due to the current inequality within skateboarding. All of our girls only lessons are suitable for all ages, all skill levels, and anyone who does not identify as male. These lessons are generally free, thank you to our funding providers.

*in this context, the term women represents anyone who self-identifies as female.

Private Skate Lessons

We also offer private lessons, with just you and the coach. The price is $50 for a 1 hour lesson, at your chosen location. You can book these through our website, or send us a message.

School Lessons

Skate Ed also operates within schools around Wellington, we are always eager to get more schools involved with our work. We are able to run skateboarding lessons at your school during lunchtime, class time, and after school. Please get in touch if this is something you would be interested in, we would be happy to provide you with more information.

Birthday Parties

Have you ever wanted a skateboarding session as a birthday party? We also offer private bookings for birthday parties, the price depends on your location. We are able to provide portable ramps, 15 skateboards with helmets and safety gear, and first aid certified coaches. Please get in touch if this is something you would be interested in, we would be happy to provide you with more information.
