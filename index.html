Skate School

Skate Ed is a female owned skate school based in Te Whanganui-a-Tara, Wellington. Our aim is to increase the provision of skateboarding lessons in the community, while creating opportunities for women and children.

Book a Class

About Us

We think it is important to positively promote skateboarding to our community, as it is a fun and effective way to participate in active play. It also helps people to build all sorts of skills, and confidence, while being in a safe environment.

Read More About Us

Our Lessons

All of our lessons are suitable for all ages, and all skill levels. Skateboards, safety pads, and helmets are available to borrow at every lesson. You can book ahead or show up on the day. However, lessons are weather permitting, so please keep an eye on our social media for weather updates.
