Skate School

Skate Ed is a female owned skate school, aiming to increase the provision of skateboarding lessons in Te Whanganui-a-Tara, Wellington.

About Us

It is important to us that skateboarding is positively promoted among our communities, as it is an effective way to participate in active play. It also helps individuals to build their own identities, and confidence, while being in a safe environment. We think it is important to be able to provide frequent skateboarding lessons for women* (and non-binary), due to the current inequality within skateboarding.

*in this context, the term women represents anyone who self-identifies as female.

Our Lessons

In order to achieve our goal, we have introduced school skateboarding programmes that run after school, during lunch time, and even in class time. These school lessons are in addition to our weekend skateboarding lessons, which take place at various skateparks in the region. Suitable for all ages and skill levels with skateboards, safety pads, and helmets available to borrow at every lesson. Book ahead, or show up on the day.
